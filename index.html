About Us

Built by people
from here.

PUREscapes was born in 2021 from a partnership between two industry veterans who spent their careers building some of the finest landscapes and outdoor spaces in Grey and Simcoe Counties.

We work on everything from backyard transformations to municipal infrastructure — with the same crew, the same standards, and the same commitment to getting it right.
